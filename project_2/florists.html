Meet Brooke!

Brooke is a floral designer in Michigan. She has been working as a florist in the Lansing area for two and a half years. She started at the local farmer's market learning all the skills and techniques it takes to be competitive in the floral industry. Recently, Brooke started her own business taking on floral projects of all sizes. In her first wedding season she scheduled seventeen successful weddings. Brooke says her favorite part of creating floral arrangements is meeting new people and making connections. She thrives on pinpointing her clients' interests and making their dreams a reality.

Brooke can be found on Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn
