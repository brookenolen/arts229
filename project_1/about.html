Hello! My name is Brooke and I am a floral designer and graphic designer. I love working with flowers and creating stunning, unique arrangements for all occasions. I have always been creative whether it be drawing, painting, crafting, or graphic design. I fell into floral design by accident, and I would have never thought I would be creating my own business designing wedding and event flowers. For years I have studied graphic design communication, and gained an extensive knowledge of color theory, design elements, and the fundamentals of art. When I was first introduced to floral design, I immediately knew it would become my passion!
